Design

As a Chassis Engineer, I designed many safety critical components of our current car. This included changing the roll cage and foot box dimensions for taller drivers and running FEA to make sure these changes still resulted in a safe chassis. Because this work was largely built upon the last design cycle, I am currently redesigning the chassis. I also designed the firewall, splash guard, and various tabs making sure that it was easy to fabricate in house and could be easily disassembled to reach other parts of the car.

Fabrication

Now that it is fabrication season, I worked to create a wooden jig so that we could tack the tubes in place for welding. I am also using the lathe and mill to create components for other sub-teams.

Leadership

As a chassis lead, I have worked with other team leads on planning the fabrication cycle, shortening from 2 semesters to 1 semester. I also made freshman orientation tasks, helping new members learn how to design, CAD, and go through a design review.
